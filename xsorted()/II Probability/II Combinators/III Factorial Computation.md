n! = product of natural numners from 1 - n
n! = 1 x 2 x 3 x ....
3! = 1 x 2 x 3 = 6

0! = 1
begative numbers have no factoring

n! = (n-1)! x n
- 6! = 5! x 6
(n + 1)! = n! x (n + 1)
- 7! = 6! x 7
Could be expanded to n + k factorings
- (n + k)! = n! x (n + 1) x (n + 2) x . . . x (n + k)
- (n - k)! = n! / [(n - k + 1) x (n - k + 2) . . . x (n - k + k)] last component = (n)
